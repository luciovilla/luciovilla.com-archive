About Me

Hi! I’m a journalist who mostly designs and codes. I've worked inside and outside the newsroom and bring a wealth of experience producing meaningful stories while thinking about the user experience. I’m passionate about journalism, curious how readers engage with stories and enjoy coming up with unique and creative ways to tell stories.

Currently, I work at The San Francisco Chronicle as an interactive producer where I mostly design and develop interactive stories, design workflows, a bit of project management. I also contribute on directing visuals and push the newsroom to explore new ways of storytelling. I’ve helped implement a style guide, ArchieML, organized more collaboration of the visual teams and helped designed some of the best digital work The Chronicle has been recognized for.

Previously, I worked in Chicago at Hoy Newspaper, a Spanish daily publication that’s part of Tribune Publishing, as a news applications developer and at The Chicago Reporter as a photojournalism fellow. During my time in Chicago, I went from being a photojournalist to a web designer/developer after realizing that I could utilize my web skills in journalism. And having a visuals background has influenced my designs and how I collaborate with visual content creators.

Aside from work, this spring I helped instruct a class on editorial web design and development at the Graduate School of Journalism in UC Berkeley. I am committed to diversifying our industry. I’m the board President of the Bay Area chapter of the National Association of Hispanic Journalists; recently listed in Editor & Publisher’s 2019 “25 Under 35: The Future Generation of Newspaper Leaders.”; co-founder of Code Latino Chicago and the Latino Techies Chicago Meetup.

I’m originally from Compton, CA, and graduated from Cal State Fullerton (Go Titans) with a Bachelor’s Degree in Photo Communications. During my time at Fullerton I was a photo editor at The Daily Titan. Prior to being a journalism major I was studying Computer Science at Cal Poly Pomona (Go Broncos) where I was a photo editor at The Poly Post for two years before I transferred to Fullerton.

Member of SND, ONA, IRE, NAHJ, AAJA (ally) and NABJ (ally).
